Contracts Made Modular

Brightline tools streamline legal drafting.

Modular Contracts

By saving and editing modular snippets of contract language, Brightline makes it easy to maintain templates for your entire firm.

Secure Contracts

Brightline is built for Mac & Windows. Modules are saved locally or shared via bank-grade, secure servers with your colleagues.

Exportable Contracts

Modules are combined to form contracts. Once you finish drafting the contract, simply export the file to PDF, Word or OpenOffice.

Built by Lawyers

Our team understands the importance of keeping your contracts up-to-date. As your law firm evolves, Brightline's platform evolves with you.
